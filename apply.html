Applying at the Baptist Theological Seminary of Zambia is Easy

The process starts with a Request for Application Form. Download a copy using the link below. Fill it out, and then send or deliver the form with a fee of K20 to our business offices. We will respond quickly and walk you through the rest of the process. We look forward to hearing from you and studying together!

Click here to download the Request for an Application Form

Does the BTSZ offer scholarships or sponsorships?

At this time, the seminary is pleased to offer two opportunities to enrolled students. (Prior to enrollment, applicants are unable to apply for these scholarships.) First, we offer some small work scholarships relating to maintenance, cleaning, and library assistance. Second, we annually offer the Leadership Scholarship. The students are challenged to write a short paper on a particular topic, and then the faculty evaluate the papers, awarding prizes to a winner and honorable mentions. The last recipient of the Leadership Scholarship received K4000!
