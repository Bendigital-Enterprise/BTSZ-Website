Our Programs

The following are the programs that we offer

Certificate In Christian Ministry

Through our Non-Residential Theological Training Institute (NRTTI), this program consists of regularly scheduled intensives. The classes provide an overview of Christian doctrine and practice.

Read More

Post Secondary

This level has two programs: The Bachelor of Theology and the Diploma of Theology. The former consists of four years of full-time study, and to enroll, the applicant must possess five O Level credits in any five subjects. The latter consists of three years of full-time study. To enroll in this program, the applicant must possess five O Level passes in any five subjects.

Read More

Masters of Theology

This program consists of three years of study. To enroll, the applicant must possess Bachelors of Theology.

Our Facilities

Come and enjoy our beautiful facilities located beside Twin Palm Road in Kabulonga, Lusaka! Not only are they a part of the school’s community life, but they are often available to the broader community of Lusaka. Rentals can be scheduled through our business office at +260966639625.

Library

With over 18,000 books and resources, our library is a hidden gem in Lusaka. Commentaries, topical books, textual resources, scholarly journals, and more are available. Library memberships are offered for only K100 a year!

Chapel

Seating over 350 people, our chapel is the perfect place to host your special event, gathering, or funeral.

Conference Centre

Our conference center facilities include dormitories, a meeting hall, bathrooms, and a kitchen.
